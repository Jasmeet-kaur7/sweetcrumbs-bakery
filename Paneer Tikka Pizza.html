PANEER TIKKA PIZZA

This Paneer Tikka Pizza is the ultimate fusion dish, bringing together smoky Indian street flavors and traditional Italian pizza elements. A crisp, golden-brown crust is slathered with a robust pizza sauce and piled high with marinated paneer (cottage cheese) cubes, crunch green bell peppers, onions, and juicy tomatoes. Melted mozzarella binds everything together into an aromatic, flavorful masterpiece.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 pre-baked 10-inch pizza crust
  • Paneer Topping: 1 cup paneer cubes, marinated in yogurt, tandoori masala, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice
  • Vegetables: ½ cup diced green capsicum, 1 medium onion (cut into squares), and 1 small tomato (chopped or rings)
  • Sauce & Cheese: ⅓ cup pizza sauce and 1.5 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Herbs & Spices: ½ tsp chaat masala, ½ tsp red chilli flakes, and fresh coriander for garnish
  • Fat: 1 tbsp olive oil or melted butter for brushing the edges

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the Setup: Preheat your baking oven to 450°F (230°C). Lightly grease your pizza pan or preheat a baking stone.
  2. Marinate and Sear: Toss the paneer cubes in your seasoned yogurt mixture. Quickly pan-sear the cubes for 2 minutes over high heat until slightly golden around the edges, then remove.
  3. Assemble the Base: Place the pizza crust on your baking pan. Brush the bare edges lightly with olive oil or melted butter to achieve a beautiful crispy texture. Spread the pizza sauce evenly, leaving a half-inch border.
  4. Layer Toppings and Cheese: Scatter a small handful of mozzarella over the sauce first. Distribute the seared paneer tikka cubes, capsicum, onions, and tomato pieces evenly across the pizza. Cover generously with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Dust with chilli flakes.
  5. Bake to Perfection: Slide into the oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes until the cheese is bubbling, gooey, and spotted with deep golden-brown patches. Top with a pinch of chaat masala and fresh coriander before slicing.
