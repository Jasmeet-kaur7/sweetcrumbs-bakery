SPICED VANILLA BEAN & CHAI LOAF

Our Spiced Vanilla Bean & Chai Loaf is a sophisticated masterclass in warm spice pairing. Infusing whole black tea leaves, aromatic cardamom, and ginger directly into a velvety sour cream batter, this deeply fragrant cake features real speckles of Madagascar vanilla bean caviar. Drapered smoothly under a dense spice glaze and adorned with star anise, it brings a luxurious tearoom ritual to your table.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chai Infusion: 2 bags premium black tea steeped in ¼ cup warm heavy cream
  • Dry Base: 2 cups unbleached flour, 1 tsp baking powder, ½ tsp baking soda, ½ tsp salt
  • Warm Botanicals: 1 tsp ground cardamom, ½ tsp cinnamon, ¼ tsp ground ginger, ¼ tsp cloves
  • Moisture Core: 3 large eggs, ¾ cup sour cream, ½ cup unsalted softened butter
  • Sweeteners: ¾ cup brown sugar, 1 whole Madagascar vanilla bean pod (scraped caviar)
  • Glaze Top: 1 cup confectioners sugar, 2 tbsp spiced milk, decorated with whole star anise

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep & Steep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a standard 9x5-inch loaf pan using heavy parchment paper. Warm the heavy cream over low heat, add the black tea leaves, and steep for 10 minutes; set aside to cool down completely.
  2. Whisk Warm Spices: In a medium bowl, thoroughly sift together the unbleached flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
  3. Cream Butter & Vanilla: Cream your softened butter with the brown sugar and scraped vanilla bean caviar using a mixer until it looks light and aerated. Beat in the eggs one by one.
  4. Combine Batter: Slowly add the dry flour blend alternately with the sour cream and the cooled, strained spice cream mixture into your butter base. Fold cleanly with a wide spatula just until integrated.
  5. Bake & Decorate: Spread the batter evenly into your loaf pan. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes until a tester toothpick comes out completely clean. Once cooled fully on a wire rack, pour your smooth spice glaze over the crown and decorate with whole star anise pods.
