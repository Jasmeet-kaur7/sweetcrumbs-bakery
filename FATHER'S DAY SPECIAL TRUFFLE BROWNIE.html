FATHER'S DAY SPECIAL TRUFFLE BROWNIE

An exclusive six-piece artisanal gift assortment of ultra-fudgy dark chocolate truffle brownies. Each individual bar is layered with smooth chocolate ganache and crowned with an edible white chocolate plaque showcasing dapper Father's Day themes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g unsalted butter & 250g premium dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 300g castor sugar & 3 large eggs
  • 100g all-purpose flour & 50g Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt (to enhance the dark chocolate profile)
  • 150g dark chocolate truffly ganache (for the top adhesive layer)
  • 6 custom edible printed white chocolate topper plaques

DIRECTIONS

  1. Melt the unsalted butter and chopped dark chocolate chunks together over a double boiler until smooth.
  2. Whisk the eggs and castor sugar vigorously in a separate bowl until pale, light, and slightly aerated.
  3. Gently fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg base until fully combined.
  4. Sift in the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and sea salt, mixing carefully just until a thick batter forms.
  5. Pour the batter into a lined rectangular baking tin and bake at 175°C (345°F) for 22-25 minutes.
  6. Allow the brownie slab to cool completely, then slice it cleanly into six equal rectangular bar pieces.
  7. Spread a thin layer of chocolate ganache over each bar and gently press a custom Father's Day white chocolate plaque on top.
