How Can We Help You?

Frequently Asked Questions about Sweet Crumbs

🎂 How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For custom birthday and celebration cakes, please place your order at least 2 to 3 days in advance. This gives Chef Sarah and Rohan enough time to design and bake your masterpiece perfectly!

🌱 Do you offer eggless or gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a dedicated selection of 100% eggless pastries and cakes daily. We also bake specialized Gluten-Free Almond Blondies, though our kitchen handles wheat products.

🚗 Do you deliver around Ludhiana?

We offer store pickup at our Kipps Market location. For custom or multi-tiered cakes, we can arrange safe, specialized vehicle delivery within Ludhiana for an extra fee. Contact us via WhatsApp to check your area!

❌ Can I cancel or modify my cake order?

Because custom bakes require ingredient prep ahead of time, adjustments or cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time.
