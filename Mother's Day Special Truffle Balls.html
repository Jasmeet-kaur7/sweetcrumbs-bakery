CHOCOLATE TRUFFLE BALLS

Our signature Mother's Day Special Truffle Balls are an exquisite masterclass in artisan chocolate confection—featuring velvety ganache centers rolled into perfect spheres and draped in premium dark and white chocolate shells. Beautifully customized into an elegant assortment, they are adorned with crackling roasted walnuts, crunchy festive sprinkles, and gourmet toppings. Indulgent, sophisticated, and perfectly portioned—they are a luxurious gesture designed to celebrate Mom with every melting bite.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Velvety Chocolate Ganache Core:

    • 300g High-Quality Dark Chocolate Bars (60% to 70% cocoa, chopped finely)
    • 240ml Heavy Whipping Cream (Minimum 35% fat content)
    • 30g Unsalted Butter (Room temperature, softened)
    • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
    • A small pinch of Sea Salt (Balances and deepens the cocoa profile)

    For the Artisan Coating & Garnishes:

    • 150g Dark Chocolate Couverture (For dipping and shell structure)
    • 100g White Chocolate Couverture (For the festive white truffles)
    • 0.5 cup Walnuts or Hazelnuts (Roasted and roughly crushed)
    • 3 tablespoons Rainbow Sprinkles / Nonpareils
    • 2 tablespoons White Chocolate Drizzle Accents

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prepare the Ganache Base: Place the finely chopped dark chocolate into a heatproof glass bowl. Heat the heavy whipping cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it just begins to simmer around the edges (do not boil). Immediately pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit undisturbed for 2 minutes.
  2. Emulsify and Chill: Stir the cream and chocolate gently with a rubber spatula from the center outward until completely uniform, glossy, and smooth. Stir in the softened butter, vanilla extract, and sea salt. Cover the surface directly with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours until firmly set.
  3. Portion and Roll: Line a large baking tray with parchment paper. Use a small cookie scoop or a tablespoon to portion out equal amounts of the chilled ganache. Quickly roll them between your palms into smooth spheres. If the ganache gets sticky or warm, pop it back into the freezer for 10 minutes.
  4. Dip the Shells: Melt your dark and white chocolate couvertures in separate small bowls using 20-second microwave bursts. Use a dipping fork to lower each truffle ball into the melted chocolate, tap off the excess, and slide it gently onto your parchment paper.
  5. Decorate the Assortment: Work quickly before the chocolate shells set! Garnish one half of the dark truffles with a generous crown of crushed roasted walnuts and the other half with white chocolate drizzles. For the white chocolate truffles, roll them directly into the rainbow sprinkles to achieve a colorful festive finish. Place them inside mini paper candy cases.
  6. Set and Store: Chill the completed assortment in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to solidify the outer shells completely. Arrange neatly into an upscale boutique box for presentation.
