CARAMEL FILLING BROWNIE

Indulge in the gold standard of desserts with our Salted Caramel Shavings Brownie. This artisanal creation features an incredibly fudgy dark chocolate base, topped with a generous layer of our signature, slow-cooked salted caramel. For the final touch, we crown each square with a mountain of hand-carved milk chocolate shavings that melt effortlessly on the tongue. It’s a sophisticated balance of sweet, salty, and velvety textures that looks as impressive as it tastes.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fudgy Base: 1/2 cup unsalted butter, 1 cup dark chocolate chips (melted), 3/4 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 2/3 cup all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup cocoa powder.
  • Salted Caramel Layer: 1 cup granulated sugar, 6 tbsp salted butter, 1/2 cup heavy cream, 1 tsp sea salt.
  • The Garnish: 4 oz block of milk or dark chocolate (for curls).

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix brownie ingredients until glossy and bake in a lined square pan for 20-22 minutes. Let cool completely.
  2. The Caramel: Melt sugar in a saucepan until golden amber. Stir in butter and cream (carefully!), then add sea salt. Let the caramel cool slightly until it reaches a thick, spreadable consistency, then pour over the brownies.
  3. The Shavings: Use a vegetable peeler or a sharp knife on the edge of a room-temperature chocolate block to create long, elegant curls.
  4. Assembly: Gently pile the chocolate curls over the set caramel layer. For the best presentation, do this just before serving so the curls stay crisp and beautiful.
