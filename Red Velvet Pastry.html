RED VELVET PASTRY

This exquisite Red Velvet Pastry features layered crimson sponge cake with a subtle hint of premium cocoa powder, balanced flawlessly by elegant, tangy cream cheese frosting. Velvety smooth, incredibly moist, and beautifully vibrant, it offers a sophisticated balance of flavors perfect for upscale dessert presentations or elegant personal treats.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake Base:

    • 400g Plain cake flour
    • 10g Unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 5g Baking soda
    • A pinch of salt
    • 115g Unsalted butter (softened)
    • 330g Caster sugar
    • 2 Large eggs
    • 250ml Vegetable oil
    • 250ml Buttermilk
    • 2 tbsp Red liquid food coloring
    • 1 tsp White vinegar
    • 2 tsp Vanilla extract

    For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

    • 400g Premium cream cheese
    • 115g Unsalted butter (softened)
    • 450g Icing sugar (sifted)
    • 1½ tsp Vanilla extract
    • Cake crumbs (for top garnish)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a rectangular baking pan with parchment paper and grease lightly.
    2. Sift Dry Mix: In a medium bowl, thoroughly sift together the cake flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
    3. Cream Wet Mix: In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter and caster sugar together until pale and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vegetable oil, vanilla extract, white vinegar, and liquid red food coloring.
    4. Combine Batter: Alternately add the sifted dry ingredients and buttermilk into the wet mixture in three batches. Mix on low speed just until combined to maintain a tender crumb structure.
    5. Bake Base: Pour the smooth red batter evenly into the prepared pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center emerges clean. Allow the cake to cool completely on a wire rack.
    6. Whip Frosting: Beat the cream cheese and remaining softened butter together until perfectly silky. Gradually sift in the icing sugar and pour in the vanilla extract, whipping until light and voluminous.
    7. Assemble Pastries: Carefully slice the cooled cake layer horizontally or into clean rectangles to create multi-tiered layers. Pipe or spread cream cheese frosting between each layer, and coat the top evenly. Garnish with fine red velvet cake crumbs and a clean piped rosette on top. Chill well before slicing into uniform pastries.
