CHILLI CHEESE SANDWICH

The Chilli Cheese Sandwich is a vibrant, Mumbai street-style classic that delivers a perfect balance of rich, melted texture and fiery flavor. This gourmet recipe features a savory stuffing made with finely grated paneer (cottage cheese), sharp processed cheese, crisp colorful bell peppers, and fresh green chillies layered inside soft, golden-toasted bread slices. It makes for an incredibly satisfying breakfast, evening snack, or high-protein tiffin option.

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread: 4 to 6 fresh white or brown bread slices
  • Paneer: 1 cup, finely crumbled or grated
  • Cheese: ½ cup mozzarella or processed cheddar, grated
  • Green Chillies: 2-3, finely chopped (adjust for heat level)
  • Vegetables: ¼ cup finely chopped capsicum (bell peppers) & onions
  • Spices & Seasoning: ½ tsp black pepper powder, ½ tsp oregano, and salt to taste
  • Spicy Spreads: 1-2 tbsp red chilli sauce or fresh green mint-coriander chutney
  • Butter: 2 tbsp softened butter for toasting

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a mixing bowl, thoroughly combine the crumbled paneer, grated cheese, chopped capsicum, onions, green chillies, black pepper, oregano, and salt. Mix until it forms a uniform, creamy stuffing.
  2. Assemble the Base: Place the bread slices on a clean surface. Evenly spread a layer of butter followed by a thin coating of green chutney or red chilli sauce on the inner sides.
  3. Stuff the Sandwich: Generously add the paneer-cheese mixture onto one slice of bread and press down gently. Close the sandwich with a second slice, keeping the spiced side facing inwards.
  4. Toast to Perfection: Heat a skillet, tawa, or sandwich grill maker over medium-low heat. Apply a dollop of butter to the outside of the sandwich and place it on the hot pan.
  5. Serve Hot: Cook for 3 to 4 minutes per side, pressing lightly with a spatula, until the outer crust turns beautifully crisp and golden brown and the cheese melts completely. Slice diagonally and serve immediately.
