MPTHER'S DAY SPECIAL TRUFFLE BROWNIE

Our signature Mother's Day Fudge Brownie Duo is an ultra-premium chocolate indulgence—expertly baked to achieve a rich, intensely dense, and fudgy core with a tight, velvety crumb. Topped with a smooth layer of rich chocolate ganache and finished with handcrafted, custom pink fondant plaques reading "Happy Mother's Day" and "MOM," this pair strikes the perfect balance of visual elegance and deep chocolate decadence.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Fudgy Chocolate Brownie Base:

    • 150g Unsalted Butter (Melted and cooled slightly)
    • 200g Granulated White Sugar
    • 100g Light Brown Sugar (Packed, for an extra chewy profile)
    • 3 Large Eggs (Room temperature)
    • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
    • 100g All-Purpose Flour (Sifted)
    • 75g Dutch-Process Cocoa Powder (High-quality for maximum flavor depth)
    • 0.25 teaspoon Fine Sea Salt
    • 100g Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips (Folded into the batter)

    For the Topping & Custom Plaques:

    • 100g Dark Chocolate Shards + 60ml Heavy Cream (Melted into a silky ganache adhesive layer)
    • White & Pink Fondant / Sugar Paste (For rolling and cutting the rectangular plaques)
    • Edible Food Coloring Pens / Custom Prints (For the "Happy Mother's Day" and "MOM" custom designs)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Whisk the Base: Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Line an 8x8 inch square baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides. Whisk the melted butter, white sugar, and brown sugar vigorously together in a large bowl. Add the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, beating for a full minute until glossy.
  2. Fold in the Solids: Gently sift the all-purpose flour, Dutch-process cocoa powder, and fine sea salt directly into the wet sugar mixture. Use a silicone spatula to fold the dry ingredients until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  3. Bake the Base: Spread the heavy batter evenly into your lined pan. Bake for 22–25 minutes. Do not overbake—a toothpick inserted into the center should come out with a few moist, fudgy crumbs attached rather than clean. Cool completely in the pan before lifting out.
  4. Apply the Ganache Adhesive: Make a quick ganache by pouring hot heavy cream over your dark chocolate shards. Stir until smooth. Spread a very thin layer over the flat top of the brownie slab to act as the perfect flavor-packed glue for your custom designs. Cut into neat rectangular bars.
  5. Craft the Custom Plaques: Roll your pink sugar paste out to a 2mm thickness. Cut into perfect matching rectangles that fit your cut brownie pieces. Use edible ink stamps, custom icing transfers, or food coloring pens to write the festive "Happy Mother's Day" and "MOM" text onto the plaques.
  6. Assemble and Display: Gently press the custom sugar plaques directly onto the wet ganache layer of the brownie bars. Place the duo side-by-side on a rustic wooden platter or inside an upscale bakery box for final presentation.
