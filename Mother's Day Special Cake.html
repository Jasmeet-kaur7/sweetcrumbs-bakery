BLACK FOREST

Our signature Mother's Day Black Forest Cake is an exquisite timeless classic—featuring layers of deep, moist chocolate sponge lightly brushed with sweet cherry syrup. Filled with velvety whipped cream and tart red cherries, the exterior is elegantly frosted, bordered with rich dark chocolate buttercream stars, and crowned with a mosaic of premium dark chocolate curls, pristine whipped rosettes, and vibrant glacé cherries. An unforgettably luxurious experience crafted to honor Mom.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Chocolate Sponge Layers:

    • 200g All-Purpose Flour (Sifted)
    • 60g Dutch-Process Cocoa Powder (High-quality for rich color and flavor)
    • 250g Caster Sugar
    • 1.5 teaspoons Baking Powder
    • 1.5 teaspoons Baking Soda
    • 1 teaspoon Fine Salt
    • 2 Large Eggs (Room temperature)
    • 240ml Whole Milk
    • 120ml Vegetable Oil
    • 240ml Strong Hot Coffee (Enhances the chocolate flavor profile)

    For the Cherry Filling & Assembly:

    • 400g Canned Dark Sweet Cherries (Drained, chopped, syrup reserved for brushing)
    • 600ml Heavy Whipping Cream (Cold, minimum 35% fat content)
    • 60g Icing Sugar (Sifted)
    • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
    • 100g Dark Chocolate Block (Shaved or curled for the top display)
    • 8-10 Whole Glacé Cherries (For the rosette garnish)
    • 50g Dark Chocolate Buttercream (For the lower star border)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Sponges: Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line two 8-inch round cake pans. Whisk together all the dry sponge ingredients in a large bowl. Add the eggs, milk, and oil, beating until smooth. Slowly stream in the hot coffee until the batter is uniform but thin. Divide between pans and bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool completely on a wire rack.
  2. Whip the Cream: In a chilled glass bowl, beat the cold heavy whipping cream, icing sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff, pipeable peaks form. Keep chilled until ready to use.
  3. Slice and Soak: Carefully slice each cooled chocolate sponge horizontally to create 3 or 4 flat layers. Place the first layer on your cake stand and brush generously with the reserved cherry syrup to keep it moist.
  4. Layer the Filling: Spread a thick layer of whipped cream over the soaked sponge, then press half of the chopped dark sweet cherries evenly into the cream. Place the next sponge layer on top and repeat the soaking and filling process. Top with the final sponge layer.
  5. Frost the Exterior: Coat the top and sides of the cake with a smooth, pristine layer of white whipped cream using an offset spatula. Smooth the edges perfectly.
  6. Garnish and Decorate: Using a star tip in a piping bag filled with remaining whipped cream, pipe 8 to 10 elegant rosettes around the upper perimeter. Top each rosette with a whole glacé cherry. Fill the open center with a heavy mountain of dark chocolate curls. Finish by piping a decorative star border around the bottom base using dark chocolate buttercream.
