MATCHA CROISSANT

Elevate your morning pastry game with this beautiful, artisan croissant. Featuring golden, crispy laminated layers dipped halfway into a vibrant, sweet matcha white chocolate glaze, this treat is finished with a generous dust of finely chopped pistachios and fresh green tea leaves for texture.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Laminated Croissant Base:

    • 500g Bread Flour
    • 60g Sugar
    • 10g Fine Sea Salt
    • 10g Instant Yeast
    • 140g Whole Milk (Cold)
    • 140g Water (Cold)
    • 280g Premium Unsalted Butter (Chilled, block for rolling)

    For the Matcha White Chocolate Glaze & Topping:

    • 150g High-Quality White Chocolate Chips
    • 2 tablespoons Culinary Grade Matcha Powder (Sifted)
    • 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil (For a glossy finish)
    • 0.25 cup Shelled Pistachios (Finely chopped for dusting)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prepare the Base Dough: Mix flour, sugar, salt, yeast, milk, and water together in a stand mixer. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Shape into a flat rectangle, wrap, and chill in the refrigerator overnight.
  2. The Butter Sheet: Pound the 280g of cold butter between parchment paper sheets into a neat rectangle that is half the length of your rolled-out dough. Keep chilled.
  3. Enclose and Laminate: Roll out your cold dough, place the butter block in the center, and fold the dough flaps over to seal it completely. Roll the dough sheet out lengthwise and perform three consecutive envelope-style tri-folds, resting the dough in the freezer for 20 minutes between each turn.
  4. Shape and Rise: Roll the final dough sheet out to about 4mm thickness. Cut out elongated triangles. Roll them up tightly from the wide base to the tip. Arrange on a baking tray and let proof at a cool room temperature for 2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Bake: Egg-wash the croissants lightly. Bake at 390°F (200°C) for 15-18 minutes until deeply golden brown and perfectly crisp. Remove and allow to cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. Glaze and Decorate: Melt the white chocolate chips and coconut oil together in a heatproof bowl. Whisk in the sifted matcha powder until vibrant green and smooth. Dip one half of each cooled croissant into the matcha glaze. Immediately sprinkle with chopped pistachios before the coating sets.
