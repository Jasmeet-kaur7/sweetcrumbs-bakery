MANGO PASTRY

The Fresh Mango Cream Pastry is a signature tropical delight featuring light, airy vanilla sponge cake soaked in a sweet mango syrup. It is layered with a rich mango diplomat cream and house-made mango jam, then topped with fluffy whipped cream and generous chunks of fresh, seasonal Alphonso mangoes.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Vanilla Sponge:

    • All-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt
    • Sugar and eggs (or egg substitute for eggless versions)
    • Vegetable oil and vanilla extract
    • Milk (for soaking and batter)

    For the Filling & Topping:

    • Fresh ripe mangoes (pureed for cream and diced for topping)
    • Heavy whipping cream and powdered sugar
    • Mango jam or compote
    • Mango syrup (for soaking the sponge)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Sponge: Whisk together dry ingredients and combine with beaten eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla. Bake in a shallow rectangular pan until golden and set. Once cool, slice into three equal layers.
  2. Prepare Mango Cream: Whip heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in fresh mango puree to create a smooth mango diplomat cream.
  3. Assemble: Lightly soak the first sponge layer with mango syrup. Spread a layer of mango jam followed by a thick layer of mango cream. Repeat for the next two layers.
  4. Finish: Frost the top with a final layer of whipped cream. Garnish with a generous amount of fresh mango chunks and optional decorative swirls.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 2–4 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld and the pastry to set properly.
