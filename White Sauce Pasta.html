WHITE SAUCE PASTA

This Creamy White Sauce Penne Pasta is an indulgent comforting classic featuring an authentic silky Béchamel sauce base. Tender tubes of penne pasta absorb a velvety roux-thickened milk mixture infused with garlic, melted cheese, and aromatic Italian herbs. Sprinkled with vibrant red chilli flakes and dried oregano, it offers a rich and deeply satisfying flavor profile.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta: 200g penne pasta
  • Roux Base: 2 tbsp unsalted butter & 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • Liquid: 1.5 cups whole milk (warm)
  • Aromatics: 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • Vegetables: ¼ cup sliced red and yellow bell peppers
  • Cheese: ¼ cup grated processed or mozzarella cheese
  • Seasoning & Herbs: 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp red chilli flakes, and salt to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil Pasta: Cook penne pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain well and set aside.
  2. Sauté Veggies: Heat a tiny splash of oil or butter in a pan. Briefly sauté the minced garlic and sliced bell peppers for 2 minutes until tender-crisp, then remove them from the pan.
  3. Make the White Sauce: Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in the same pan over low heat. Add the all-purpose flour and whisk continuously for 1 to 2 minutes until a smooth roux forms without browning.
  4. Thicken the Sauce: Gradually pour in the warm milk in a slow stream while whisking vigorously to prevent lumps. Simmer on low heat until the sauce thickens and smoothly coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Assemble and Serve: Stir the sautéed veggies, boiled penne, and grated cheese into the sauce. Season with salt, dried oregano, and red chilli flakes. Toss well on low heat for 1 minute until the cheese completely melts. Serve hot.
