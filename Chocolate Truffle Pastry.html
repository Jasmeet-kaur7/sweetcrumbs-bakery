CHOCOLATE TRUFFLE PASTRY

This decadent Chocolate Truffle Pastry is an indulgent multi-layered dessert. It features layers of moist, dense chocolate sponge cake infused with light sugar syrup. The layers are sandwiched between rich, velvety chocolate truffle ganache. Topped with a glossy mirror glaze and elegant dark chocolate curls, it delivers the ultimate chocolate experience.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Chocolate Sponge:

    • 200g All-purpose flour
    • 60g Premium unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1½ tsp Baking powder
    • ½ tsp Baking soda
    • 200g Granulated sugar
    • 2 Large eggs
    • 120ml Whole milk
    • 120ml Warm water or brewed coffee
    • 60ml Vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

    For the Truffle Ganache & Topping:

    • 400g Dark chocolate (couverture, chopped)
    • 300ml Heavy whipping cream (35% fat)
    • 30g Unsalted butter (room temperature)
    • 50ml Sugar syrup (for soaking)
    • Dark chocolate fans/curls (for decoration)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Sponge: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Whisk the dry ingredients together, then beat in the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Stir in the warm water or coffee last. Pour into a lined square or round baking pan and bake for 25 minutes. Cool completely.
  2. Prepare the Ganache: Heat the heavy cream until it just starts to simmer. Pour it over the chopped dark chocolate. Let it sit for 2 minutes, then stir gently until smooth and glossy. Mix in the butter to add shine. Let the ganache cool until it reaches a spreadable, pipeable consistency.
  3. Slice and Soak: Slice the cooled chocolate sponge horizontally into three even, neat layers. Lightly brush each layer with sugar syrup to keep the pastry intensely moist.
  4. Assemble the Pastry: Place the bottom sponge layer down. Spread a thick, uniform layer of chocolate ganache over it. Repeat the process for the second and top layers. Smooth the remaining ganache over the top and sides of the cake.
  5. Decorate and Chill: Pour a thin layer of remaining warm fluid ganache over the top for a smooth finish. Pipe elegant chocolate decorative swirls or add dark chocolate filigree designs on top. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before cutting into sharp, clean slices.
