BUTTREY WHIPPED SHORTBREAD COOKIES

Our signature whipped shortbread cookies are a masterclass in texture—whipped to an ultra-light, airy perfection that produces a delicate, melt-in-the-mouth heart. Refined, classic, and unapologetically elegant—they are not just a simple biscuit; they are an exquisite melt-away experience dressed in a festive crown of colorful confections.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Unsalted Butter (Must be softened perfectly at room temperature)
  • 0.5 cup Powdered Icing Sugar (Sifted to prevent lumps)
  • 1.5 cups All-Purpose Flour (Sifted)
  • 0.5 cup Cornstarch (The secret ingredient for a delicate, melt-away texture)
  • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 0.25 teaspoon Fine Salt (Omit if using salted butter)
  • 2 tablespoons Rainbow Sprinkles or Nonpareils (For the classic decorative top)

DIRECTIONS

  1. The Core Whip Phase: In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, place your softened unsalted butter and sifted powdered icing sugar. Whip on a medium-high speed for a full 8 to 10 minutes. The mixture must turn completely pale white and double in volume, resembling fluffy frosting.
  2. Add Fragrance: Stream in the pure vanilla extract and salt while continuing to beat on a low speed until just fully incorporated.
  3. Introduce the Flour Base: Turn the mixer speed to low. Gradually add the sifted all-purpose flour and cornstarch into the bowl. Mix until a very soft, light dough forms. Do not overmix during this final step, or the cookie structure will turn tough.
  4. Portion and Dress: Roll the delicate dough into small, uniform 1-inch balls. Arrange them onto a baking sheet lined with clean parchment paper. Press down gently on the center of each dough sphere with the flat tines of a fork to create traditional ridges, then scatter a pinch of rainbow sprinkles directly into the indented wells.
  5. The Crucial Chill: Transfer the entire baking tray into your refrigerator for at least 30 to 45 minutes before baking. Chilling solidifies the whipped butter, ensuring the cookies retain their light structure and fork ridges perfectly in the oven.
  6. Bake with Care: Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Bake the chilled cookies for 12 to 14 minutes. Watch them closely—you want the cookies fully cooked through and set, but they must remain perfectly pale cream without turning golden brown on top.
  7. Cool to Set: Let the delicate cookies settle on the hot baking tray for 5 to 10 minutes right out of the oven. Transfer them very gently with a thin spatula to a wire cooling grid to cool completely before storing in an airtight tin.
