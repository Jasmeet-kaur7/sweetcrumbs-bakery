ALMOND FRANGIPANE & SUMMER BERRY CAKE

Our Almond Frangipane & Summer Berry Cake is a classic European heritage recipe executed with rustic elegance. Ground almond flour creates an incredibly moist, dense, and nutty frangipane sponge layer, which is richly loaded with a vibrant baked-in mix of fresh field berries. Crowned with velvet whipped cream, sweet strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and a heavy scattering of toasted flaked almonds, it offers a refreshing symphony of flavors.

INGREDIENTS

  • Frangipane Sponge Base: 1 cup blanched superfine almond flour, ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • Fats & Sweetener: ½ cup unsalted cream butter (softened), ¾ cup fine white castor sugar
  • Binding & Lift: 3 large eggs, 1 tsp baking powder, and ¼ tsp sea salt
  • Fruit Medley: 1 ½ cups mixed fresh summer berries (strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • Topping Layers: 1 cup heavy whipping cream (beaten to stiff peaks), 1 tbsp powdered sugar
  • Texture Finish: ⅓ cup sliced raw flaked almonds (toasted crisp), dusting of icing sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Baking Tin: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line your standard loaf baking pan cleanly with non-stick parchment paper, leaving generous side handles for easy removal later.
  2. Cream the Base: In a deep mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and castor sugar until light, fluffy, and pale yellow. Beat in the eggs one by one until smoothly combined.
  3. Fold Nut Flour: Gently fold the superfine almond flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and sea salt into the wet mixture. Fold until smooth, then delicately swirl half of the fresh field berries directly into the heavy batter.
  4. Bake & Cool: Pour the dense batter into your pan. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes until the cake turns deep golden-brown on top and a toothpick inserted into the thickest center comes out clean. Transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely.
  5. Assemble the Crown: Once completely cold, pipe or spread the sweet vanilla whipped cream across the entire top surface. Heavily scatter the remaining fresh summer berries and toasted almond flakes. Finish with a gentle dusting of powdered icing sugar before slicing and serving.
