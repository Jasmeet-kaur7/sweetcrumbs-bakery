MORNING CRUFFIN & LATTE DUET

Our Morning Cruffin & Latte Duet is the definition of artisanal cafe luxury. Hand-crafted using micro-laminated pastry technique, our signature cruffin folds premium butter into layered flakes, filled with a subtle jam core and finished with crushed roasted nuts. Served alongside a velvety, double-shot cafe latte boasting flawless microfoam art, it turns your fast morning rush into an intentional sensory ritual.

INGREDIENTS

  • Laminated Cruffin Base: 2 ½ cups unbleached bread flour, 1 cup cold European-style butter (82% fat), ½ cup cold milk, yeast
  • Sweet Fill & Crunch: ¼ cup wild berry fruit compote injection core, 2 tbsp crushed toasted hazelnuts
  • Latte Foundations: 18g freshly ground single-origin espresso beans, 6 oz velvety steamed whole milk
  • Glaze Finish: 1 tbsp light simple honey syrup brushed onto the pastry crust crown

DIRECTIONS

  1. Laminate the Pastry: Prepare an enriched yeast dough, roll flat, and enclose your cold block of premium butter. Perform a series of envelope folds and turns, chilling the dough thoroughly between each turn to lock in dozens of paper-thin buttery layers.
  2. Shape & Proof: Roll out the laminated pastry dough thin, slice into vertical strips, roll them up tight, and slice them lengthwise. Nest the swirled layers directly into greased muffin tins. Let rise at room temp for 2 hours until puffy.
  3. Bake Pastries: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the cruffins for 22 to 25 minutes until heavily puffed, deeply caramelized, and flaky. Remove and let cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes.
  4. Inject Filling & Garnish: Use a pastry piping tip to inject a small dollop of berry compote into the hollow core of each warm cruffin. Brush the top ring with honey syrup and scatter a handful of crushed toasted hazelnuts.
  5. Pull the Espresso & Steaming: Pull a rich double espresso shot into a ceramic cup. Simultaneously steam cold whole milk to a glossy, tight microfoam texture at 140°F (60°C). Free-pour the velvet foam over the espresso base to form a clean leaf design and serve immediately alongside the warm pastry.
