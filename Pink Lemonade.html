PINK LEMONADE

Pink Lemonade is a vibrant, thirst-quenching classic twist on traditional lemonade. It balances the sharp tartness of fresh lemon juice with a delicate sweetness, sporting a signature pink hue derived from natural berry juices, botanical infusions, or fruit syrups.

INGREDIENTS

  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice (from 4-5 medium lemons)
  • 100 grams granulated sugar (or simple syrup to taste)
  • 750 ml chilled filtered water
  • 60 ml pure cranberry, raspberry, or strawberry juice (for color and subtle flavor)
  • Fresh lemon wheels, blueberries, and herb sprigs (for garnish)
  • Crushed ice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Combine granulated sugar and 120 ml of water in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely to make a simple syrup. Let it cool.
  2. In a large serving pitcher, mix together the freshly squeezed lemon juice, the cooled simple syrup, and the remaining chilled water.
  3. Stir in the cranberry, raspberry, or strawberry juice gradually until the liquid hits your desired shade of pink.
  4. Fill serving glasses generously with crushed ice.
  5. Pour the pink lemonade over the ice.
  6. Garnish each glass with a fresh lemon wheel, a couple of floating berries, and a small sprig of thyme or mint.
