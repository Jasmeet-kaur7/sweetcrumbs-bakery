FETTUCCINE PASTA

This Fettuccine Alfredo is a rich, luxurious Italian-American staple known for its silky-smooth texture and comforting flavor profile. Long, flat ribbons of fettuccine pasta are perfectly swirled in an emulsion of melted unsalted butter, rich heavy cream, and a generous coating of freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Elegantly finished with freshly cracked black pepper and a sprinkle of chopped parsley, this dish is pure decadence on a plate.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta: 250g dried fettuccine pasta
  • Butter: ½ cup unsalted butter (good quality)
  • Cream: 1 cup heavy cream or whipping cream
  • Cheese: 1 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan), freshly grated
  • Aromatics: 2 cloves garlic, finely minced (optional)
  • Seasoning: ½ tsp freshly cracked coarse black pepper and salt to taste
  • Garnish: 2 tbsp fresh Italian parsley, finely chopped

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil the Fettuccine: Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil and season heavily with salt. Cook the fettuccine ribbons for 8 to 10 minutes until perfectly al dente. Reserve ½ cup of starchy pasta water, then drain the noodles.
  2. Melt and Infuse: In a large skillet or frying pan, melt the unsalted butter over medium-low heat. Add the minced garlic if using, and sauté gently for 1 minute until fragrant without allowing it to brown.
  3. Simmer the Cream: Pour the heavy cream into the skillet with the melted butter. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  4. Emulsify the Alfredo Sauce: Turn the heat down to low. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta directly into the skillet. Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese across the noodles. Toss rapidly using tongs, pouring in 2 to 3 tablespoons of the reserved warm pasta water to help the cheese melt smoothly into a velvety glaze.
  5. Season and Serve: Turn off the heat completely. Keep tossing until the sauce tightly clings to every ribbon. Season with salt and coarse black pepper. Transfer to warm serving plates, garnish with extra Parmesan and fresh chopped parsley, and serve immediately.
