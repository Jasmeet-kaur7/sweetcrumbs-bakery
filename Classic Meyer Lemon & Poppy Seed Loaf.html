CLASSIC MEYER LEMON & POPPY SEED LOAF

Our Classic Meyer Lemon & Poppy Seed Loaf offers a bright, sun-drenched indulgence crafted to elevate your sensory tea routine. Infused with the fresh aromatic zest and cold-pressed juice of ripe Meyer lemons, this tender, velvety crumb is accented by a subtle, delicate crunch of blue poppy seeds. It is finished with a sweet-tart, translucent citrus glaze that cuts through rich tea profiles beautifully.

INGREDIENTS

  • Batter Foundation: 2 cups unbleached pastry flour, 1 cup fine castor sugar, 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Citrus Infusion: Freshly grated zest and juice of 3 organic Meyer lemons
  • Texture Seed: 2 tbsp premium blue poppy seeds
  • Moisture Core: 3 large eggs, ½ cup melted unsalted butter, and ½ cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • Aromatics & Lift: 1 tsp pure vanilla bean extract, ¼ tsp sea salt flakes
  • Zesty Glaze: 1 cup sifted powdered sugar mixed smoothly with 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Oven & Baking Tin: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a standard 9x5-inch loaf baking pan cleanly with non-stick parchment paper, allowing an overhang on the sides for easy lifting.
  2. Blend Dry Mix & Seeds: In a large mixing bowl, thoroughly sift together the pastry flour, baking powder, and sea salt. Stir in the premium blue poppy seeds until evenly distributed.
  3. Combine Wet Base: In a separate bowl, rub the fresh lemon zest directly into the castor sugar with your fingertips to release the essential citrus oils. Whisk in the eggs, melted butter, sour cream, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until completely uniform.
  4. Fold and Incorporate: Gently pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients. Use a wide rubber spatula to fold the batter together just until the flour disappears, making sure not to overmix the base.
  5. Bake & Drizzle Glaze: Pour the uniform batter into your loaf pan. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes until a tester toothpick comes out completely clean. Cool fully on a wire rack before drizzling the glossy lemon glaze across the entire top crown. Garnish with a slice of fresh lemon and a mint leaf.
