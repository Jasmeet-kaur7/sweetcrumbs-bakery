RED SAUCE PASTA

This premium Red Sauce Pasta delivers an authentic Mediterranean flavor profile right to your dinner table. Perfectly cooked penne rigate is folded into a thick, vibrant sauce made from fresh vine-ripened tomatoes, sweet garlic, onion flakes, and extra virgin olive oil. Garnished with a medley of Italian herbs, fresh green basil leaves, and a sprinkle of sharp cheese, it offers a beautifully balanced tangy and savory taste.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta Base: 250g penne or fusilli pasta
  • Fresh Tomatoes: 4 large ripe red tomatoes (blanched and pureed)
  • Aromatics: 1 medium onion (finely chopped) and 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Herb Seasoning: 1 tbsp dried Italian seasoning (oregano, rosemary, thyme)
  • Spice Adjustments: 1 tsp red chilli flakes and ½ tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • Sauce Enhancers: 2 tbsp tomato paste (for deep color) and ½ tsp sugar (to balance acidity)
  • Finishing Touches: 2 tbsp olive oil, ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese, and 5-6 fresh basil leaves

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil the Noodles: Bring a deep pot of water to a rolling boil with 1 tablespoon of salt. Add the penne pasta and cook for 9 minutes until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain the noodles cleanly.
  2. Sauté the Base: Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Sauté the minced garlic and chopped onions for 3 to 4 minutes until they are soft, sweet, and fragrant.
  3. Simmer the Rich Tomato Sauce: Pour in the fresh tomato puree and tomato paste. Mix thoroughly. Add the dried Italian seasoning, red chilli flakes, black pepper, sugar, and salt. Cover and simmer on medium-low heat for 12 minutes until the oil starts separating from the sauce.
  4. Combine and Glaze: Drop the cooked penne pasta directly into the bubbling red sauce. Pour in 3 tablespoons of the reserved starchy pasta water. Toss continuously on high heat for 1 minute until the sauce thickens into a glossy glaze that grips the pasta.
  5. Plate and Garnish: Turn off the flame. Stir in the torn fresh basil leaves and half of the grated cheese. Transfer to a serving platter, top with the remaining cheese, and serve instantly while steaming hot.
