EGG SALAD

This Classic Egg Salad Sandwich is the ultimate lunchtime comfort food. It features a creamy, well-seasoned egg salad filling made from perfectly hard-boiled eggs mashed with rich mayonnaise, a touch of tangy mustard, and fresh green herbs. Layered generously between wholesome whole-grain oat bread with crisp frilly lettuce and ripe tomato slices, it balances soft, creamy, and crunchy textures beautifully.

INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs: 4 large eggs, hard-boiled and cooled
  • Creamy Base: 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Tangy Kick: 1 tsp Dijon mustard or yellow mustard
  • Herbs & Crunch: 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh chives or dill, and 1 tbsp finely minced celery (optional)
  • Seasoning: ¼ tsp paprika, salt, and freshly cracked black pepper to taste
  • Bread: 4 slices of whole-grain oat bread or your favorite sandwich loaf
  • Fresh Produce: 4 large curly green lettuce leaves and 4 ripe tomato slices

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Eggs: Peel the hard-boiled eggs and place them in a medium mixing bowl. Use a fork or an egg chopper to coarsely mash them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix the Filling: Add the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, chopped chives/dill, minced celery, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the mashed eggs. Stir gently until thoroughly combined and creamy.
  3. Build the Base: Place the slices of whole-grain bread on a clean surface. Layer two large pieces of curly lettuce on the bottom slices to protect the bread from getting soggy.
  4. Layer the Fillings: Place two slices of fresh tomato over the lettuce layer. Divide the egg salad mixture evenly and spoon it over the tomatoes, smoothing it out to the edges.
  5. Assemble and Serve: Top with the remaining curly lettuce leaves and close the sandwiches with the top slices of bread. Press down gently, slice in half if desired, and serve immediately or chill for later.
