CHEESE PIZZA

This Margherita Pizza is a timeless Italian favorite boasting a beautifully blistered, golden-brown crust topped with an indulgent blend of melted cheeses. A crisp thin crust provides the base for a rich, zesty tomato pizza sauce layer, completely covered in gooey, bubbling mozzarella and sharp cheddar highlights. Sliced into perfect triangular wedges, it delivers an authentic pizzeria experience right from your home oven.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 pre-baked 10-inch thin pizza crust (or fresh pizza dough)
  • Pizza Sauce: ⅓ cup robust, herb-infused tomato pizza sauce
  • Cheese Blend: 1 cup shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese and ¼ cup grated mild cheddar
  • Aromatics: ¼ tsp garlic powder or finely minced garlic
  • Herbs & Spices: ½ tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp red chilli flakes, and fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Fat: 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil for brushing the crust edges

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you are using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating to ensure a crispy bottom crust.
  2. Prepare the Crust: Place your pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza peel. Lightly brush the outer edges of the crust with extra virgin olive oil to help it turn deep golden brown during baking.
  3. Spread the Sauce Base: Ladle the tomato pizza sauce into the center of the crust. Using the back of a spoon, smooth the sauce outward in circles, leaving a half-inch border around the edge. Sprinkle garlic powder evenly over the sauce.
  4. Layer the Cheese: Generously scatter the shredded mozzarella and grated cheddar cheese mixture over the sauce layer, ensuring full, even coverage right up to the sauce line. Dust the top with dried oregano.
  5. Bake and Slice: Slide the pizza into the hot oven. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until the cheese is melted, bubbling, and spotted with golden brown patches. Remove from the oven, let cool for 2 minutes, slice cleanly into wedges, and serve hot.
