CHEESECAKE SWIRL BROWNIE

Elevate your dessert experience with our Artisanal Marble Cheesecake Brownies. These aren't just treats; they are edible art. We start with a foundation of our signature, ultra-dark chocolate brownie batter and hand-swirl it with a rich, tangy New York-style cheesecake infusion. Each square is uniquely marbled and finished with a scattering of premium semi-sweet chocolate morsels, offering a complex flavor profile that balances sweetness with a sophisticated, creamy tang.

INGREDIENTS

  • Premium Cocoa Base: 1/2 cup melted butter, 1 cup cane sugar, 2 farm-fresh eggs, 1 tsp Madagascar vanilla, 1/2 cup sifted flour, 1/3 cup Dutch-process cocoa.
  • Cream Cheese Infusion: 8 oz full-fat cream cheese (softened), 1/3 cup sugar, 1 egg yolk, 1/2 tsp vanilla bean paste.
  • Topping: 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips.

    • DIRECTIONS

    1. The Foundation: Prepare the dark chocolate batter and spread 3/4 of it into your lined baking tin, smoothing the surface for an even base.
    2. The Velvet Layer: Whip the cream cheese mixture until it's silk-smooth and airy, then pour it gently over the chocolate base.
    3. The Art of the Swirl: Drop small spoonfuls of the remaining chocolate batter onto the white cheese layer. Using a thin skewer, pull the dark batter through the light in a "figure-eight" motion to create the professional marble effect seen in our photos.
    4. The Perfect Bake: Generously top with chocolate chips and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes. To achieve the cleanest, most "bakery-perfect" squares, let them set in the refrigerator overnight before slicing.
