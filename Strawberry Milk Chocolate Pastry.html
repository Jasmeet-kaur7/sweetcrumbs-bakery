STRAWBERRY MILK CHOCOLATE PASTRY

This tempting Strawberry Milk Chocolate Pastry perfectly balances fruity brightness and creamy chocolate notes. It features rich chocolate sponge cake sheets layered with sweet strawberry compote and silky milk chocolate ganache cream. Topped with a smooth glaze, piped milk chocolate rosettes, and vibrant strawberry gel peaks, it delivers a stunning bakery-window presentation.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cocoa Sponge & Strawberry Layer:

    • 180g All-purpose flour
    • 45g Natural cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp Baking powder
    • ½ tsp Baking soda
    • 180g Granulated sugar
    • 2 Large eggs
    • 100ml Milk
    • 50ml Melted butter
    • 200g Fresh strawberries (finely chopped)
    • 40g Sugar + 1 tsp lemon juice (for compote)

    For the Milk Chocolate Ganache & Garnish:

    • 300g High-quality milk chocolate (chopped)
    • 200ml Heavy cream (35% fat)
    • 25g Unsalted butter
    • 50g Smooth strawberry gel/piping jelly
    • Fresh strawberry slices (for serving side-plates)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Cocoa Sponge: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Whisk flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and sugar. Whisk in eggs, milk, and melted butter. Pour the smooth batter into a lined square baking pan. Bake for 22–25 minutes. Cool completely.
  2. Simmer Strawberry Compote: Combine chopped strawberries, 40g sugar, and lemon juice in a small saucepan. Simmer over medium heat for 8 minutes until thickened and jam-like. Cool to room temperature.
  3. Make Milk Chocolate Ganache: Heat heavy cream until steaming. Pour over chopped milk chocolate. Let rest for 1 minute, then stir smoothly. Whisk in the butter. Set aside a small portion to chill until firm enough for piping. Keep the rest spreadable.
  4. Assemble the Tiers: Slice cooled sponge horizontally into three thin layers. Place the base layer down, spreading a thin layer of milk chocolate ganache, followed by a layer of strawberry compote. Lay down the middle sponge and repeat. Place the top sponge layer down.
  5. Glaze and Decorate: Pour remaining fluid ganache cleanly over the top layer for a glassy finish. Let chill for 1 hour. Cut into neat square pastries using a warm knife. Use a star tip to pipe milk chocolate rosettes on top. Alternating with the chocolate, pipe pointed dollops of vibrant strawberry gel.
