SIGNATURE BRANDED MACARONS

Our Signature Branded Macarons bring an elite level of custom luxury to the patisserie table. Meticulously made using fine almond flour meringue, each delicate cookie shell is baked to achieve flawless, smooth ruffed 'feet' and a tender, melt-in-your-mouth bite. Custom stamped with our signature 'Sc' monogram and whimsical chocolate-chip cookie illustrations, these elegant vanilla-bean infused gems sandwich a rich, silky white chocolate ganache core. Perfect for luxury dessert tables or bespoke gifting packages.

INGREDIENTS

  • Meringue Shells: 1 ¾ cups ultra-fine blanched almond flour, 1 ½ cups confectioners icing sugar, 3 large egg whites (aged at room temp), ¼ cup fine castor sugar
  • Velvet Ganache Filling: ½ cup premium white chocolate couverture chunks, 3 tbsp heavy whipping cream, 1 tsp Madagascar vanilla bean paste
  • Aromatic Profiles: A pinch of cream of tartar (for structural stability), a tiny pinch of fine sea salt
  • Bespoke Branding Art: Food-grade edible ink stamps (warm sepia and dark cocoa tones)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Sift and Prep: Process your blanched almond flour and icing sugar together in a food processor until microscopic. Sift the mixture twice through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any residual lumps to guarantee perfectly smooth, level shell surfaces.
  2. Whip the Meringue: Whip your aged egg whites with a pinch of cream of tartar until frothy. Gradually feed in the castor sugar one tablespoon at a time, whipping continuously until the meringue holds firm, glassy, stiff peaks.
  3. The Macaronage Technique: Gently fold your dry sifted almond sugar mix into the stiff egg meringue in three stages. Use a rubber spatula to cut down the center and scrape around the bowl until the thick batter thins into a glossy, slow-flowing 'molten lava' consistency.
  4. Pipe and Rest: Transfer batter to a piping bag fitted with a medium round tip. Pipe clean 1.5-inch circles onto a silicone baking mat or parchment-lined sheet. Rap the tray firmly against the counter 3-4 times to clear trapped air bubbles. Let the shells sit completely undisturbed at room temperature for 45 minutes until a dry, matte 'skin' forms on the surface.
  5. Bake, Stamp, and Fill: Bake in a preheated oven at 300°F (150°C) for 14 to 16 minutes until the cookie structures develop uniform ruffed 'feet' and peel off the sheet easily. Cool entirely. Carefully apply your custom 'Sc' and cookie-art edible ink stamps onto the tops. Pipe a rich dollop of your chilled white chocolate vanilla ganache onto a flat bottom shell, sandwich smoothly with a stamped top shell, and refrigerate for 24 hours to mature the textures before serving.
