MANGO FROSE

Our signature Mango Frosé is a vibrant, frost-kissed celebration of tropical summer—featuring succulent, sun-ripened mangoes blended to a velvety slush texture with crisp, chilled rosé notes and a touch of organic honey syrup. Crowned with fragrant, freshly plucked mint leaves, this beautifully icy elixir delivers a clean, revitalizing finish that balances fruit sweetness with sharp, sophisticated cooling.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Fresh Mango Cubes (Frozen solid for the ultimate frosty thickness)
  • 1 cup Fresh Mango Purée (Chilled Alphonso or Kesar variety)
  • 250ml Premium Non-Alcoholic Rosé (Or standard dry pink Rosé wine for an alcoholic variant)
  • 1.5 tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice (Cuts the sweetness for a sharp balance)
  • 1-2 tablespoons Honey or Simple Syrup (Adjust based on mango sweetness)
  • 1 cup Crushed Ice (To lock in the thick, slow-melting texture)
  • Fresh Mint Sprigs (For a fragrant herbal garnish)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Flash-Freeze the Fruit: If using fresh mangoes, dice them into small cubes and layout onto a tray lined with parchment paper. Freeze for at least 2 hours until rock-solid. Using pre-frozen fruit provides a smooth, thick slushy consistency without watering down the drink with too much ice.
  2. Prep the Base: Pour the chilled rosé beverage, fresh mango purée, honey syrup, and fresh lemon juice into a high-powered blender.
  3. Incorporate the Frozen Element: Add the solid frozen mango cubes and crushed ice directly into the blender container.
  4. Blend to Velvet Slush: Secure the lid tightly and blend on a high-speed setting for 45 to 60 seconds. Use a tamper tool if necessary to push down the frozen pieces until the mixture is uniform, thick, and perfectly creamy with no ice chunks left.
  5. Pour and Garnish: Immediately divide the vibrant golden slush into two elegant, chilled stemmed wine or cocktail glasses. Pat a fresh sprig of mint between your palms to release the aromatic essential oils, and place it gently on top of the frosé mountain. Serve immediately with a wide straw.
