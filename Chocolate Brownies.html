CHOCOLATE BROWNIE

Elevate your dessert experience with our Raspberry Chocolate Brownies. Each square is a masterpiece of texture—boasting a dense, fudgy heart and a delicate crackle-thin crust. Topped with hand-picked, tart raspberries and a signature dark chocolate drizzle, these brownies offer a sophisticated balance of rich cocoa and refreshing berry notes. Perfect for birthdays, romantic evenings, or as an artisanal gift that tastes like heaven.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/3 cup Premium Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt & 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 cup Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips (for batter & drizzle)
  • 1 cup Fresh Raspberries

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8-inch square pan with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Wet: In a large bowl, whisk melted butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla, beating until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  3. Add Dry: Sift in flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Gently fold until just combined—do not overmix. Fold in half the chocolate chips.
  4. Bake: Pour batter into the pan and bake for 22-27 minutes. The center should still have a slight jiggle. Let cool completely.
  5. Finish: Melt the remaining chocolate chips. Arrange fresh raspberries on top of the brownies and generously drizzle with the molten chocolate.
