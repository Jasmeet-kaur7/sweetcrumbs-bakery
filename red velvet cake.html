RED VELVET CAKE

"This Premium Red Velvet Cake is the height of elegance. It features a vibrant, velvety-textured top that feels as soft as it looks. Wrapped in a smooth white chocolate collar and finished with delicate handcrafted sugar daisies, it is the perfect choice for birthdays, anniversaries, or any special celebration"

INGREDIENTS

For the Red Velvet Base:

  • 2 ½ cups Cake Flour
  • 1 ½ cups Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 2 tsp Red Food Coloring Gel

For the Decoration:

  • The Wrap: 200g White Chocolate (melted and set into a band)
  • The "Velvet" Top: Red Cocoa Butter Spray or fine cake crumbs
  • The Flowers: White Fondant or sugar paste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake a standard red velvet sponge and let it cool.
  2. Spray the top with red velvet cocoa spray for the fuzzy look.
  3. Wrap the bottom half in a smooth white chocolate band.
  4. Top with 3 small fondant daisies for a clean, elegant finish.
