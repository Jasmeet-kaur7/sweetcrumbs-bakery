CHOCOLATE CAKE

"A moist, deep-cocoa sponge finished with a smooth chocolate glaze. (12 servings)"

INGREDIENTS

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Drip:

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake: Mix dry and wet ingredients, then bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
  2. Frost: Let the cake cool and add your frosting.
  3. Drip: Pour melted chocolate over the edges.
  4. Decorate: Add chocolate chips on top!
