BLOOD ORANGE JALAPENO SPRITZER

Our signature Spicy Passion Fruit Mocktail is an exotic, tantalizing symphony of bold flavors—featuring tropical, tangy passion fruit nectar shaken with a crisp hint of cooling cucumber and a calculated kick of fresh jalapeño heat. Poured over an icy mountain of crushed ice and crowned with beautiful, golden edible pansies, this multi-layered elixir strikes the ultimate, sophisticated balance between sweet, tart, and heat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 0.5 cup Fresh Passion Fruit Nectar or Pulp (Strained, seeds removed)
  • 2-3 thin slices Fresh Jalapeño Pepper (Remove seeds for a milder warmth)
  • 3-4 thin slices Fresh English Cucumber (Brings a cooling baseline)
  • 15ml Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 15ml Organic Agave Nectar or Simple Syrup (To balance the high acidity)
  • 120ml Chilled Sparkling Water or Ginger Ale (For a lively, effervescent fizz)
  • 1.5 cups Crushed Ice
  • Bright Edible Flowers & Cucumber Rounds (For the signature artisan garnish)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Muddle the Aromatics: In the base of a heavy cocktail shaker, place the thin jalapeño slices and cucumber rounds. Drizzle the agave nectar over them. Use a cocktail muddler to gently press the ingredients for 15-20 seconds to release the spicy capsaicin oils and refreshing cucumber juices.
  2. Shake with Fruit Bases: Pour the fresh passion fruit nectar and lime juice directly into the shaker. Add a handful of ice cubes, secure the lid tightly, and shake vigorously for 10-15 seconds until the outside of the shaker container frosts over.
  3. Prepare the Glassware: Pack a clean, stylish wavy tumbler glass heavily with crushed ice. Slip a couple of fresh cucumber rounds flat against the interior glass walls for a beautiful botanical look.
  4. Strain and Pour: Double-strain the vibrant pinkish-orange liquid through a fine-mesh strainer over the fresh crushed ice to remove any unwanted jalapeño seeds or pulp fragments.
  5. Add Top Fizz & Garnish: Top off the glass with a splash of chilled sparkling water or ginger ale for a lively bubble. Carefully place your bright edible flowers right on top of the ice mountain. Serve immediately with a sustainable straw.
