⭐⭐⭐⭐"Rich Cheese"

A summer staple lunch. The splash of balsamic glaze cuts through the rich cheese beautifully. I used ciabatta and it held the juices perfectly without getting soggy.

— Reviewed by David W.

PRESSED TOASTED SANDWICH

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread: 4 slices of sourdough, ciabatta, or thick Italian bread
  • Cheese: 6 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced thick
  • Tomatoes: 1 large ripe vine tomato, sliced thin
  • Herbs: ½ cup fresh whole basil leaves
  • Spreads: 2 tbsp traditional basil pesto sauce
  • Condiments: 1 tbsp thick balsamic glaze drizzle
  • Fat: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil or softened butter for pressing

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Slices: Lay your bread slices on a clean cutting board. Spread a thin, even layer of basil pesto sauce across the inner sides of all slices.
  2. Layer the Filling: On two of the bread slices, arrange the thick mozzarella cheese slices. Layer the sliced tomatoes on top of the cheese, then cover generously with fresh whole basil leaves.
  3. Add Finishers: Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the basil leaves. Season lightly with salt and black pepper if desired. Close the sandwiches with the remaining bread slices.
  4. Press the Sandwich: Brush the outer tops and bottoms of the sandwiches lightly with olive oil or butter. Place onto a hot preheated panini press or grill pan over medium heat.
  5. Toast and Serve: Close the press lid firmly (or weigh down with a heavy skillet if using a pan). Cook for 4 to 5 minutes until the bread displays dark golden grill lines and the mozzarella begins to ooze out nicely. Slice diagonally and serve immediately.
