BRIOCHE CINNAMON KNOTS

Our Artisanal Cinnamon Cardamom Knots are an exquisite, hand-braided take on classic Scandinavian baking traditions. Made from a rich, slowly proofed yeast dough infused with crushed green cardamom seeds, each bun is layered with a dark, aromatic cinnamon sugar butter filling. Baked to a flawless golden brown and finished with a scattering of crunchy Swedish pearl sugar, they deliver an unbeatable warmth and texture to your morning table.

INGREDIENTS

  • Enriched Yeast Dough: 3 cups strong bread flour, 1 packet active dry yeast, ¼ cup melted butter
  • Dough Infusion: 1 cup warm whole milk, 1 tsp freshly crushed green cardamom seeds, ¼ cup sugar
  • Aromatic Filling: ½ cup softened unsalted butter, ½ cup organic brown sugar, 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • Texture Topping: ¼ cup premium imported Swedish pearl sugar
  • Glaze Binder: 2 tbsp simple sugar syrup (brushed right out of the oven)
  • Binding Core: 1 large farm egg (for the smooth egg wash coating)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Proof the Yeast Base: Dissolve the active dry yeast and one tablespoon of sugar in warm whole milk. Let it sit undisturbed for 5 to 10 minutes until it becomes beautifully frothy and active.
  2. Knead and Rise: Combine your proofed yeast mix with the bread flour, remaining sugar, crushed cardamom, and melted butter. Knead vigorously for 8-10 minutes until a smooth, elastic dough forms. Cover and let rise in a warm spot for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  3. Roll & Fill: Roll the risen dough out on a floured surface into a large, even rectangle. Spread the softened cinnamon-spiced brown butter filling smoothly across the entire surface. Fold the dough into thirds like a letter.
  4. Cut and Braid Knots: Cut the folded dough into vertical strips. Twist each individual strip tightly around your fingers and loop the loose ends through the center to form an elegant, twisted braided knot. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet and rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Bake & Decorate: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush each knot lightly with an egg wash and heavily coat with the crunchy Swedish pearl sugar flakes. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes until rich, golden, and deeply aromatic. Brush immediately with simple syrup upon removal.
