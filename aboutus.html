Our Story

Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Welcome to Sweet Crumbs Bakery, where every recipe tells a story and every bite feels like home. What started as a passion project in a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery built on love, laughter, and a lot of flour.

For our founder, Neha, the kitchen has always been the heart of the home. Founded in 2026, Sweet Crumbs was born from the belief that baking isn't just about mixing ingredients—it’s about creating moments of joy and tradition.

Today, we bring that same warmth and care to everything we bake. We still do things the time-honored way: cracking fresh eggs, measuring real butter, and baking completely from scratch every single morning. We take a strict, "no shortcuts" approach in our kitchen.

We believe that the best moments in life are sweetened by handcrafted treats. We are truly honored to be a part of your daily routines and special milestones.

Stop by, catch a whiff of our fresh morning bakes, and taste the handcrafted difference today!
