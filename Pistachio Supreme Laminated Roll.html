PISTACHIO SUPREME LAMINATED ROLL

Our Pistachio Supreme Laminated Roll—inspired by the viral New York pastry sensation—is a masterclass in modern viennoiserie. Circularly coiled from dozens of paper-thin, buttery layers of laminated croissant dough, it is baked inside ring molds to archive a crisp, golden crust on both sides while remaining tender within. The hollow core is generously injected with a velvety, house-crafted pistachio praline cream, dipped in an elegant white chocolate glaze, and finished with crushed raw Sicilian pistachios.

INGREDIENTS

  • Laminated Pastry Shell: 3 cups premium T55 French flour, 1 cup cold dry butter (84% butterfat blocks), ¼ cup cold whole milk, organic cane sugar, instant yeast
  • Bespoke Pistachio Filling: 1 cup heavy whipping cream, ½ cup artisanal 100% pure pistachio paste, 3 large egg yolks, ¼ cup fine sugar, a pinch of sea salt
  • Glaze Dipping Top: ½ cup premium white chocolate couverture, 2 tbsp heavy cream, a touch of natural green botanical coloring (optional)
  • Garnish Crunch: ⅓ cup raw Sicilian pistachios (shelled, toasted, and finely chopped)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Laminate the Viennoiserie Dough: Mix your enriched milk dough and let it rest. Enclose your flat block of premium cold dry butter into the center of the dough sheet. Execute three precision single folds, letting the layered sheet chill thoroughly in the refrigerator for 45 minutes between turns to create hundreds of distinct buttery layers.
  2. Roll, Cut, and Shape: Roll out the laminated dough to a 1/2-inch thickness. Slice into long, even 1-inch strips. Roll each strip tightly into a spiral wheel shape. Place the spirals flat inside well-greased circular English muffin rings or pastry molds on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Let proof at room temperature for 2 hours until doubled in size and puffy.
  3. Prepare the Pistachio Custard: Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale. Heat heavy cream until steaming, then steadily temper it into your egg mixture while whisking. Cook over medium-low heat until thickened into a dense pastry cream. Remove from heat, vigorously beat in the 100% pure pistachio paste and sea salt, then chill fully in a piping bag.
  4. Bake Under Weights: Place a sheet of non-stick parchment paper and a second heavy baking tray directly on top of the proofed pastry rings to compress them into flat, even circular discs. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25 to 30 minutes until both sides are perfectly level and golden brown. Remove rings and let cool completely.
  5. Inject, Glaze, and Garnish: Poke two small holes in the top of each cooled circular roll. Deeply pipe the chilled pistachio cream into the core until heavily filled. Melt your white chocolate glaze components together, dip the top third of each roll into the glaze, and immediately cover with a generous dusting of chopped toasted pistachios.
