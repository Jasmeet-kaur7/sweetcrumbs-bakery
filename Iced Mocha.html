ICED MOCHA

A decadent, refreshing cold coffee drink that beautifully combines robust espresso with rich chocolate syrup and chilled milk. Served over ice and piled high with whipped cream and chocolate drizzle, it is the perfect treat for chocolate and coffee lovers alike.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 standard double shot of espresso (approx. 60 ml, cooled slightly)
  • 2 tablespoons high-quality chocolate syrup or chocolate sauce
  • 180 ml chilled milk (whole milk or oat milk work best)
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (for topping)
  • Extra chocolate syrup (for glass decoration and drizzle)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Drizzle a tablespoon of chocolate syrup along the inside walls of a tall glass for presentation.
  2. Fill the decorated glass generously with ice cubes.
  3. In a separate small container, mix the freshly brewed espresso with 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup until completely smooth.
  4. Pour the chilled milk over the ice cubes in your serving glass.
  5. Gently pour the chocolate-infused espresso mixture over the milk.
  6. Top with a generous swirl of whipped cream and finish with a final drizzle of chocolate syrup.
