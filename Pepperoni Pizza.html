PEPPERONIC PIZZA

This Pepperoni Pizza is a quintessential favorite, highlighting a crisp, golden crust loaded with savory toppings. It features a bright, well-seasoned tomato sauce layer under an abundant blanket of stringy, melted mozzarella cheese. Copious slices of premium spicy pepperoni cover the top, crisping up beautifully into signature "cups" during baking to capture the rich oils and bold spices.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Crust: 1 fresh 12-inch pizza dough ball or pre-made crust
  • Sauce: ½ cup garlic and herb tomato pizza sauce
  • Cheese: 1.5 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Meat: ¾ cup thinly sliced premium beef or pork pepperoni
  • Seasoning: ½ tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp red chilli flakes, and ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • Finishers: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil and 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Crank your oven up to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, ensure it heats thoroughly inside for at least 30 minutes before baking.
  2. Prep the Crust: Stretch or roll the pizza dough out to a 12-inch disc over a piece of parchment paper or a floured pan. Lightly brush the perimeter edge with olive oil.
  3. Assemble Foundations: Spread the herb tomato sauce evenly across the base with the back of a ladle, keeping the oiled edge clear. Evenly distribute 1 cup of the shredded mozzarella over the sauce.
  4. Arrange the Pepperoni: Lay the pepperoni slices in a single layer over the cheese, slightly overlapping them since they shrink when cooking. Scatter the remaining mozzarella over the top, along with dried oregano, garlic powder, and chilli flakes.
  5. Bake and Crisp: Slide the pizza into the hot oven. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until the crust turns an appetizing deep golden brown, the cheese is bubbling vigorously, and the pepperoni edges crisp up perfectly. Slice into triangles and finish with grated Parmesan.
