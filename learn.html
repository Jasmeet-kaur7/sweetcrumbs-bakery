Our Baking Philosophy

At Sweet Crumbs, we believe that bread and pastries should be made the old-fashioned way: with patience, premium ingredients, and plenty of love.



Premium Sourcing

We choose only the finest ingredients for our daily bakes. From premium dark chocolate slabs to organic vanilla extracts and high-protein local flour, we never cut corners on quality. No artificial preservatives are ever added.

Custom Celebration Cakes

Planning a birthday, wedding, or anniversary in Ludhiana? We design customized masterworks tailored to your exact flavor and theme profile. Because everything is handmade from scratch, we require a minimum 3 to 5 days lead time for all custom cake bookings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 : How should I store my artisan sourdough?
A: Keep it in a paper bag or a wooden breadbox at room temperature for up to 3 days. Avoid keeping bread in plastic bags or refrigerators, as it speeds up drying.

Q2 : Do you offer allergen-free options?
A: We offer custom eggless, gluten-free, and vegan recipe varieties on demand. However, please note our facility processes wheat, dairy, and nuts daily.
