VEGGIE PIZZA

This Supreme Garden Veggie Pizza is a colorful, nutrient-packed rustic pie boasting an incredible assortment of fresh toppings. Slices of tender mushrooms, crisp red bell peppers, sharp red onion slivers, and rich black olives are layered over a robust tomato base and gooey melted mozzarella cheese. Garnished with aromatic fresh basil leaves and coarse spices, it delivers an earthy, satisfying, and high-fiber gourmet profile.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 fresh 12-inch rustic pizza dough crust
  • Sauce: ½ cup garlic-herb tomato pizza sauce
  • Cheese: 1.5 cups premium low-moisture shredded mozzarella
  • Garden Vegetables: ½ cup sliced button mushrooms, ½ cup julienned red bell peppers, and ¼ cup thin red onion slices
  • Toppings: ¼ cup sliced black olives and fresh green basil leaves
  • Finishers: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, ½ tsp oregano, and ¼ tsp crushed red pepper flakes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Setup: Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up thoroughly.
  2. Roll the Crust: Stretch the pizza dough over a sheet of parchment paper or a floured work surface. Lightly brush the outer edges with olive oil for a deep golden finish.
  3. Sauce and Cheese Foundations: Spread the garlic-herb tomato sauce across the base, leaving a half-inch border. Scatter 1 cup of the mozzarella evenly over the sauce layer.
  4. Arrange the Veggies: Layer the sliced mushrooms, red bell peppers, red onion slivers, and black olives across the cheese. Sprinkle the remaining ½ cup of mozzarella over the toppings to bind them down.
  5. Bake and Garnish: Bake for 12 to 15 minutes until the rustic crust edges are crisp and brown and the cheese is fully melted and bubbling. Top immediately with fresh torn basil leaves, oregano, and red pepper flakes before cutting into wedges.
