PASTA PIZZA

This Pasta Pizza is a glorious carbs-on-carbs fusion delicacy popularized by street food culture and fast-food chains. A thick, fluffy baked pizza crust serves as the foundation for an abundant topping of perfectly cooked, ridge-cut macaroni pasta drenched in a luscious, spicy pink cream sauce. Blanketed with massive layers of stringy, melting mozzarella cheese, every slice yields an incredible cheese pull and a rich, deeply comforting bite.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 thick-crust pre-baked 10-inch pizza base
  • Pasta: ½ cup dried macaroni or penne pasta (boiled until al dente)
  • Sauce Blend: ¼ cup pizza sauce mixed with ¼ cup rich white Béchamel or heavy cream
  • Cheese Layer: 1.5 cups shredded processed mozzarella or pizza cheese blend
  • Veggies: ¼ cup sliced red onions and chopped capsicum
  • Seasoning: 1 tsp Italian mixed herbs, 1 tsp red chilli flakes, and ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • Fat: 1 tbsp softened butter or olive oil for brushing

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil and Coat Pasta: Cook your macaroni noodles in salted boiling water until soft. Drain cleanly, then toss the hot pasta directly into your mixed pink pizza-cream sauce blend until heavily coated.
  2. Prepare the Crust: Preheat your baking oven to 450°F (230°C). Lay the pizza base down on a clean tray and brush the rim thoroughly with butter or olive oil for a glossy, golden bake.
  3. Layer the Cheese Base: Spread a thin layer of pure pizza sauce on the crust, then scatter half of the shredded mozzarella across the base to help hold the top layers.
  4. Load the Pasta Topping: Generously spoon the saucy macaroni pasta evenly over the cheese. Scatter the sliced onions and capsicum chunks among the pasta tubes, then cover the entire surface completely with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Dust the top with chilli flakes and Italian herbs.
  5. Bake and Serve: Slide the tray into the hot oven. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until the cheese melts completely, bubbling up into a gorgeous golden brown crust. Slice immediately to experience the ultimate hot cheese pull!
