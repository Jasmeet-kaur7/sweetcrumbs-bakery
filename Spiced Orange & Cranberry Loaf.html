SPICED ORANGE & CRANBERRY LOAF

This Spiced Orange & Cranberry Loaf combines a ultra-moist, buttery crumb infused with aromatic citrus oil and rich warming spices. Bursting with sweet, tart whole cranberries throughout each slice, it is finished with a vibrant orange-infused white glaze and decorated with sugared winter fruit and fresh orange rounds. It serves as a beautiful brunch centerpiece or an elegant holiday gift.

INGREDIENTS

  • Batter Base: 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Citrus & Fruit: Zest and fresh juice of 2 large oranges, 1 ½ cups fresh or frozen cranberries
  • Spices: ½ tsp ground cinnamon, ¼ tsp ground ginger, and a pinch of ground cloves
  • Moisture: 3 large farm eggs, ½ cup unsalted melted butter, and ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • Glaze: 1 cup powdered confectioners sugar mixed with 2 tbsp fresh orange juice
  • Garnish: Fresh sliced orange wheels, frosted cranberries, and a sprig of fresh rosemary

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Oven & Pan: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a standard 9x5-inch loaf pan and line it securely with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and ground cloves. Gently toss the cranberries into the dry mix to coat them evenly.
  3. Whisk the Wet Mix: In a separate bowl, vigorously whisk together the sugar, fresh orange zest, orange juice, eggs, melted butter, and Greek yogurt until completely smooth.
  4. Combine & Assemble: Gently fold the wet liquid mixture into the dry flour and cranberry mixture using a rubber spatula just until combined. Pour evenly into your prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake & Cool: Bake for 50 to 55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool inside the pan for 10 minutes, then lift out onto a wire rack to cool fully before pouring the sweet orange glaze over the top.
