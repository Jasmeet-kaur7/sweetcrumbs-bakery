COCONUT MACAROONS

Our signature pistachio coconut macaroons are a masterclass in confection—expertly baked to yield a chewy, golden coconut mound studded with finely chopped, roasted pistachios. Complete with a luxurious dark chocolate dipped base and a delicate artful drizzle across the top—they are not just a simple biscuit; they are an exquisite interplay of nutty crunch, tropical sweetness, and velvet chocolate crafted to elevate your sweet indulgence.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups Sweetened Shredded Coconut
  • 1 cup Salted, Roasted Pistachios (Finely chopped, plus extra for garnish)
  • 1 can (14 oz) Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 2 Large Egg Whites (Room temperature)
  • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 0.5 teaspoon Pure Almond Extract (Complements the earthy pistachio aroma)
  • 1 cup High-Quality Dark Chocolate Chips (Melted, for dipping and drizzling)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep Work: Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Line two large baking sheets with heavy-duty parchment paper to prevent the sticky condensed milk from catching.
  2. Combine the Base: In a large mixing bowl, toss together the shredded coconut and finely chopped roasted pistachios until evenly distributed. Pour in the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Stir thoroughly until a thick, sticky cookie batter forms.
  3. Whip the Egg Whites: In a separate clean glass bowl, beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the dense coconut mixture using a rubber spatula to lighten up the texture.
  4. Portion and Shape: Using a 1.75-inch ice cream scoop or a well-rounded tablespoon, portion the sticky mixture into small mounds onto your prepared baking sheets. Leave about an inch of space between each macaroon.
  5. Bake Until Golden: Bake in the center of the preheated oven for 18–22 minutes. The macaroons should be beautifully golden-brown on the outside while remaining moist and chewy in the centers. Remove and let cool on the pan for 5 minutes before moving them onto a wire cooling rack.
  6. The Chocolate Finish: Melt your dark chocolate chips in a small microwave-safe bowl in short 20-second increments. Dip the flat bottoms of each cooled macaroon directly into the melted chocolate, letting any excess drip back down. Set the cookies bottom-side down back onto parchment paper.
  7. Drizzle and Garnish: Spoon the remaining melted chocolate into a small piping bag with a tiny tip snipped off. Drizzle lines across the top domes back and forth, then immediately finish with a tiny pinch of crushed pistachios. Transfer the tray to the refrigerator for 30 minutes to set the chocolate layer before serving.
