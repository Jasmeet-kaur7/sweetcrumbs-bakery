MINT CREAM BROWNIE

Experience the ultimate chill with our Mint Chocolate Ganache Brownies. These striking three-layer bars begin with a dark, fudgy brownie foundation, followed by a lush, silk-smooth mint buttercream center. The finale is a thick, hand-poured layer of rich chocolate ganache that snaps perfectly with every bite. It's a sophisticated, refreshing indulgence that looks as stunning as it tastes—perfect for those who love the classic pairing of mint and cocoa.

INGREDIENTS

  • The Brownie Base: 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup flour, 1/3 cup cocoa powder.
  • The Mint Layer: 2 cups powdered sugar, 1/4 cup softened butter, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tsp peppermint extract, a drop of green food coloring.
  • The Ganache Top: 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips, 6 tbsp unsalted butter.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Base: Bake your standard brownie batter in an 8x8 pan at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes. Let it cool **completely** before adding layers.
  2. Mint Cream: Beat the powdered sugar, butter, milk, and peppermint extract until light and fluffy. Spread evenly over the cooled brownies. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to set.
  3. Ganache: Melt chocolate chips and butter together in a microwave or double boiler until glossy. Pour over the chilled mint layer and smooth with a spatula.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. For those razor-sharp layers shown in the photo, slice while cold using a hot, dry knife.
