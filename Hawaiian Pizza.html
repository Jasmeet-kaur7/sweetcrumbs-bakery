HAWAIIAN PIZZA

This Hawaiian Pizza perfectly hits the spot with its iconic sweet and savory flavor fusion. A thick, chewy crust base is lined with zesty tomato sauce and layered with sweet pineapple chunks, smoky protein pieces, and thinly sliced red onions. Smothered in bubbly mozzarella cheese and baked to a deep golden crisp, this classic pie proves why the fruit and cheese pairing remains a crowd favorite.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 fresh 12-inch pizza dough ball or pre-baked crust
  • Sauce: ½ cup traditional herb tomato pizza sauce
  • Cheese: 1.5 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Fruit: ¾ cup pineapple chunks (canned, drained well, or fresh)
  • Toppings: ½ cup diced ham slices (or smoky vegan bacon bits/paneer chunks) and ¼ cup thinly sliced red onion rings
  • Finishers: 1 tbsp cornmeal (for dusting the pan) and ½ tsp red chilli flakes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat your Setup: Preheat your kitchen oven to 450°F (230°C). Lightly dust your metal baking sheet or hot pizza stone with a sprinkle of cornmeal.
  2. Shape the Crust: Roll or stretch your pizza dough out on a floured surface to form a uniform circle. Transfer it carefully onto your prepared baking tray.
  3. Layer the Foundations: Spread a smooth, even layer of the herb tomato sauce across the base, leaving a neat half-inch boundary around the edge. Scatter half of the mozzarella cheese across the sauce.
  4. Distribute the Toppings: Evenly scatter the drained pineapple chunks, protein bits, and sliced red onion rings over the cheese layer. Top with the remaining mozzarella and a sprinkle of chilli flakes.
  5. Bake Until Crispy: Place the tray into the preheated oven. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes until the outer crust turns deep golden brown and the cheese layer bubbles beautifully with scattered toasted spots. Slice and serve hot.
