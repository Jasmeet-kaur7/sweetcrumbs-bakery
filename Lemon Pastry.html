LEMON PASTRY

This Cream Cheese & Lemon Pastry is a bright, refreshing dessert featuring tangy, lime-infused mousse and creamy cream cheese. These luscious layers are sandwiched between airy vanilla sponge cake and finished with a smooth white chocolate glaze, citrus swirls, and delicate edible floral decorations.

INGREDIENTS

For the Vanilla Sponge:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Baking powder and a pinch of salt
  • Whole milk and vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract

For the Lemon Cream Cheese Filling:

  • Full-fat cream cheese, softened
  • Fresh lemon juice and zest
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Powdered sugar

For the Glaze & Garnish:

  • White chocolate chips
  • Lemon curd for swirling
  • Yellow food coloring (optional)
  • Edible fondant flowers and chocolate petals

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Sponge: Mix dry and wet ingredients for the vanilla sponge. Bake in a rectangular pan at 350°F (175°C) until golden. Once cooled, slice into three even layers.
  2. Prepare the Filling: Beat the cream cheese, lemon juice, zest, and powdered sugar until smooth. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks and gently fold it into the cream cheese mixture.
  3. Assemble: Place the first sponge layer down and spread a thick, even layer of the lemon cream cheese mousse. Repeat with the remaining layers.
  4. Glaze: Melt white chocolate with a splash of cream to create a smooth ganache. Pour over the top layer. Use lemon curd to create decorative swirls while the glaze is still wet.
  5. Set and Serve: Refrigerate for at least 4 hours to allow the mousse to set. Slice into rectangular pastries and garnish with chocolate petals and fondant flowers before serving.
