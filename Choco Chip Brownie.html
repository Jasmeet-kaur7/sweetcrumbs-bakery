CHOCO CHIP BROWNIE

Our Double Chocolate Chunk Brownies are a purist's dream. Crafted for the ultimate chocolate lover, these bars feature a dense, gooey center and that coveted, tissue-thin crinkle crust. We’ve folded in a generous amount of premium semi-sweet chocolate chips that melt into molten pockets of joy during baking. It’s a timeless, handcrafted classic that delivers pure, unadulterated comfort with every decadent square.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/3 cup High-Quality Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips (plus extra for topping)

DIRECTIONS

  1. The Foundation: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8 inch baking pan. Whisk the melted butter and sugar together in a large bowl.
  2. The Emulsion: Add eggs and vanilla. Whisk vigorously for 1-2 minutes—this is the secret to getting that beautiful "shiny, crackle top" shown in the photo.
  3. The Dry Mix: Gently fold in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Do not overmix! Stop as soon as the last streak of flour disappears to keep them fudgy.
  4. The Chocolate: Stir in the chocolate chips. Pour the batter into the pan and sprinkle a handful more chips on top for that artisanal look.
  5. The Bake: Bake for 22-25 minutes. They should be slightly underbaked in the center for maximum gooiness. Let them cool completely before slicing to ensure clean edges.
