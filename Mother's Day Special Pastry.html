RASPBERRY MOUSSE ENTREME BARS

Our signature Mother's Day Raspberry Entremet Bars are a breathtaking masterclass in classical French patisserie—precision-layered to display a light vanilla biscuit foundation topped with an ultra-silky raspberry mousse and a striking, glossy ruby gelée mirror glaze. Elegantly crowned with smooth white chocolate cream dollops, fresh plump raspberries, and a delicate touch of micro-herbs—they offer an exquisite, tart, and refreshing flavor experience tailored for Mom.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Delicate Sponge Base (Joconde/Biscuit):

    • 3 Large Egg Whites (Room temperature)
    • 30g Caster Sugar
    • 100g Ground Almond Flour (Sifted)
    • 100g Icing Sugar
    • 3 Large Whole Eggs
    • 30g All-Purpose Flour
    • 20g Unsalted Butter (Melted and cooled)

    For the Silky Raspberry Mousse Core:

    • 250g Fresh Raspberry Purée (Strained to remove all seeds)
    • 60g Caster Sugar
    • 2.5 teaspoons Gelatin Powder (Bloomed in 3 tablespoons cold water)
    • 250ml Heavy Whipping Cream (Whipped to soft peaks)

    For the Ruby Gelée Glaze & Garnish Display:

    • 150g Clear Raspberry Juice or Coulis
    • 40g Sugar
    • 1 teaspoon Gelatin Powder (Bloomed in 1 tablespoon cold water)
    • 100ml White Chocolate Chantilly Cream (For the teardrop dollops)
    • 1 cup Fresh Whole Raspberries
    • A few sprigs of Fresh Micro-Herbs or Mint (For a vibrant botanical contrast)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Bake the Base: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Whip egg whites and caster sugar to stiff peaks. In a separate bowl, beat icing sugar, ground almonds, and whole eggs for 5 minutes. Fold in all-purpose flour, melted butter, and the meringue. Spread onto a sheet pan and bake for 6–8 minutes. Let cool.
    2. Build the Mousse: Heat 100g of the raspberry purée and sugar in a small pan until simmering. Stir in the bloomed gelatin until melted, then mix back into the remaining cold purée. Allow to cool to room temperature, then gently fold into the softly whipped heavy cream.
    3. Assemble the Layers: Line a rectangular pastry frame or silicone mold with a cut sheet of your cooled sponge. Pour the raspberry mousse smoothly over the sponge base. Smooth the top flawlessly with a bench scraper and freeze for 2 hours to solidify.
    4. Apply the Glossy Gelée: Heat your clear raspberry juice and sugar until warm. Dissolve the bloomed gelatin into the liquid. Cool completely to room temperature, then pour a thin, perfect mirror layer over the frozen mousse layer. Return to the freezer for an additional 4 hours or overnight.
    5. Slice clean Bars: Unmold the frozen entremet slab. Using a warm, clean chef's knife, slice the large sheet into elegant, narrow rectangular bars. Wipe the blade clean between every single slice to keep the pristine layers sharp.
    6. Garnish and Serve: Arrange the cut bars onto a pristine white serving plate. Use a round piping tip to place smooth teardrop dollops of white chocolate cream down the bar center, alternating with fresh raspberries stuffed with tiny dots of gelée.
