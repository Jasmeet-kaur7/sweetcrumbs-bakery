ARTISANAL FRESH FRUIT DANISH

Our Artisanal Fresh Fruit Danish is a delicate celebration of premium multi-layered laminated pastry. Hand-folded using cold European-style butter, the square puff outer crust bakes to a magnificent flaky crunch and glistening golden shine. A central pool of silk-textured vanilla bean pastry cream anchors a vibrant mosaic of fresh fruit toppings, including ripe strawberries, kiwi rounds, and mandarin segments, offering an exquisite sweet contrast.

INGREDIENTS

  • Laminated Danish Dough: 3 cups bread flour, 1 cup unsalted butter (chilled blocks), ¼ cup sugar, 1 packet dry yeast, ½ cup whole cold milk
  • Silky Custard Base: 1 cup heavy cream, 3 large egg yolks, ¼ cup sugar, 1 tbsp cornstarch, 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • Fresh Orchard Glaze: ½ cup fresh strawberries, 2 ripe kiwis (peeled and sliced), ½ cup mandarin orange slices
  • Glistening Topping Finish: ¼ cup apricot jam glaze (warmed and strained) for a beautiful high-gloss mirror finish

DIRECTIONS

  1. Laminate the Pastry: Knead your milk-enriched yeast dough, flatten, and cold-lock a rolled block of premium butter into the core. Perform three sets of tri-fold envelope folds, allowing the pastry sheets to chill thoroughly in the refrigerator between sets to construct hundreds of microscopic, flake-producing layers.
  2. Cook the Custard Pastry Cream: Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch until pale. Bring your heavy cream and vanilla bean paste to a bare simmer, then steadily stream it into the eggs while whisking. Return to the stove and cook over medium heat until a dense, velvety custard forms. Chill completely.
  3. Cut and Fold: Roll out the chilled laminated pastry dough to a ¼-inch thickness. Slice into clean 4x4-inch squares. Fold the outer corners inwards toward the center or roll the edges up slightly to create a strong protective retaining wall framework. Place on a parchment-lined pan and rise for 45 minutes.
  4. Assemble and Bake: Preheat your convection oven to 400°F (205°C). Spoon a generous dollop of chilled vanilla custard right into the hollow core center of each risen pastry square. Brush the exposed pastry dough framework edges with a light egg wash. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes until heavily puffed, highly layered, and deep golden brown.
  5. Glaze and Fruit Garnish: Allow your baked pastries to cool slightly for 10 minutes. Arrange a decorative mosaic pattern of fresh strawberry halves, kiwi slices, and mandarin segments directly over the baked custard surface. Lightly brush the warm apricot glaze over the fruit and pastry crust for a sparkling presentation.
