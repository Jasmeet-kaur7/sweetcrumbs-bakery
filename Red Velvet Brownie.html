RED VELVET BROWNIE

Indulge in the vibrant allure of our Red Velvet Cream Cheese Brownies. These stunning bars offer a unique, tight-crumb texture that is both fudgy and velvety, infused with premium cocoa and a hint of classic buttermilk tang. Each square is crowned with a thick, hand-whipped layer of artisanal cream cheese frosting and a sprinkle of crimson cake crumbs. It’s a sophisticated twist on a classic favorite, designed to be the centerpiece of any celebration.

INGREDIENTS

  • Red Velvet Base: 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted), 1 cup granulated sugar, 2 large eggs, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/4 tsp salt, 1 tsp red food coloring, 3/4 cup all-purpose flour.
  • Cream Cheese Frosting: 4 oz softened cream cheese, 1/4 cup softened butter, 1.5 cups powdered sugar, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract.
  • Garnish: Reserved red velvet brownie crumbs.

DIRECTIONS

  1. The Base: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Whisk melted butter and sugar, then add eggs, vanilla, cocoa, salt, and food coloring. Fold in flour until just combined.
  2. Bake: Pour into a lined 8-inch square pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes. **Crucial:** Let them cool completely in the pan before frosting to prevent melting.
  3. The Frosting: Beat softened cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla, whipping until light and airy.
  4. The Finish: Spread the frosting generously over the cooled brownies. Crumble any trimmings from the edges over the top for that professional, artisanal look. Chill for 30 minutes for easier slicing.
