MARGHERITA PIZZA

This Margherita Pizza is a timeless Italian masterpiece celebrating simplicity and premium ingredients. A thick, beautifully raised golden-brown artisan crust is topped with a bright tomato sauce, thick rounds of melted fresh mozzarella cheese, and slices of juicy red vine tomatoes. Crowned with fragrant scattered leaves of fresh green basil and a drizzle of fine olive oil, it proudly mirrors the colors of the Italian flag.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pizza Base: 1 fresh 10-inch artisan pizza dough round
  • Sauce Base: ⅓ cup crushed San Marzano tomato puree
  • Cheese: 6 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced into thick rounds or torn into chunks
  • Fresh Produce: 1 ripe vine-ripened tomato, thinly sliced into rounds
  • Garnish: A large handful of fresh green basil leaves
  • Finishers: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, and a pinch of sea salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven and Stone: Crank your oven to its highest setting, ideally 500°F (260°C). Place a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet inside to heat up thoroughly.
  2. Shape the Dough: Roll or stretch your fresh artisan pizza dough into a 10-inch circle on a piece of parchment paper, keeping the outer borders slightly thicker to form a nice rim.
  3. Apply the Foundation: Spread the crushed tomato puree evenly across the dough using the back of a spoon, leaving an outer edge clear. Sprinkle with a tiny pinch of sea salt.
  4. Layer the Toppings: Arrange the fresh mozzarella cheese rounds and thin tomato slices evenly over the tomato sauce layer. Lightly drizzle half of the extra virgin olive oil across the toppings.
  5. Bake and Garnish: Slide the pizza onto the preheated stone. Bake for 7 to 9 minutes until the crust edge turns beautifully puffy and golden-brown and the mozzarella melts into creamy pools. Remove from the oven, scatter the fresh basil leaves on top immediately, drizzle with the remaining olive oil, slice, and serve.
