THE ROASTED HAZELNUT & PRALINE LOAF

This Roasted Hazelnut & Praline Loaf offers a masterful balance of rich texture and buttery depth. Featuring a moist brown-butter sponge marbled with an artisanal chocolate praline swirl, each loaf is generously blanketed in thick chocolate spread and finished with a spectacular crown of hand-roasted, halved hazelnuts. It serves as an decadent addition to afternoon coffee or a beautiful centerpiece for elegant hosting.

INGREDIENTS

  • Cake Batter: 2 cups pastry flour, 1 cup unsalted butter (browned), ¾ cup golden cane sugar
  • Praline Swirl: ½ cup premium dark chocolate couverture, 3 tbsp smooth hazelnut paste
  • Nuts & Crunch: 1 cup whole hazelnuts (slow-roasted and halved)
  • Moisture & Lift: 3 large eggs, ½ cup whole cream milk, 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • Aromatics: 1 tsp pure vanilla bean extract, ½ tsp sea salt flakes
  • Topping Paste: 4 tbsp smooth chocolate gianduja spread

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep the Oven & Toast Nuts: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Roast the whole hazelnuts on a sheet pan for 8-10 minutes until aromatic, rub off the skins, cut them in half, and set aside. Line a 9x5-inch loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. Brown the Butter: Melt the unsalted butter in a saucepan over medium heat, swirling constantly until it turns a deep amber color and gives off a nutty fragrance. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Whisk Batter Base: In a large bowl, beat the cooled brown butter with the golden cane sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, followed by the milk and vanilla bean extract.
  4. Fold Dry Ingredients: Sift the pastry flour, baking powder, and sea salt directly into the wet mixture. Fold gently with a spatula until a smooth batter forms. Melt your dark chocolate praline mixture separately and ripple it through the batter to create a marbled effect.
  5. Bake & Garnish: Pour the marbled batter into the loaf pan. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the center emerges clean. Let the loaf cool completely on a wire rack before spreading a rich layer of chocolate gianduja over the top and embedding the roasted halved hazelnuts.
