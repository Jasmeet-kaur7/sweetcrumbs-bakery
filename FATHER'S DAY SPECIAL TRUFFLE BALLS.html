FATHER'S DAY SPECIAL TRUFFLE BALLS

Make Father's Day unforgettable with our signature 16-piece luxury chocolate truffle box. Featuring a diverse assortment of hand-rolled milk, white, and dark chocolate truffles complete with custom textures, dusting, and drizzles surrounding a center custom chocolate plaque.

INGREDIENTS

  • 300g premium dark chocolate chips (70% cocoa)
  • 200g high-quality milk chocolate & 100g white chocolate chunks
  • 250ml heavy whipping cream (for the velvety ganache center)
  • 50g unsalted butter (at room temperature)
  • Assorted toppings: Cocoa powder, crushed hazelnuts, sea salt crystals, and colored chocolate drizzles
  • 1 central edible printed white chocolate message plaque

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat heavy whipping cream in a small saucepan until it just begins to simmer, then pour it instantly over your dark and milk chocolate chips in separate bowls.
  2. Let the mixtures stand for 2 minutes before stirring gently until a smooth, shiny ganache forms. Stir in small cubes of unsalted butter.
  3. Place the ganache in the refrigerator for 2 hours to firm up.
  4. Use a small cookie scoop to portion out the chilled ganache, then roll rapidly between your palms to form smooth, round balls.
  5. Divide the balls into batches and coat them with custom finishes: dip some in melted milk chocolate with colorful contrast lines, roll others in cocoa powder, or toss them in fine hazelnut dust.
  6. Arrange the finished truffle balls neatly inside your grid box frame.
  7. Carefully set the custom 'Happy Father's Day' edible white chocolate plaque directly in the center compartment of the tray.
