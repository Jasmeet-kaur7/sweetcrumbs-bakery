CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Our signature cookies are a masterclass in texture—crisped to perfection at the edges with a dense, decadently chewy heart that melts at the first touch. Rich, comforting, and unapologetically indulgent—they’re not just a sweet treat; they’re an exquisite moment of pure, nostalgic bliss in every single bite.

INGREDIENTS

  • 0.75 cup Unsalted Butter (Melted and cooled slightly)
  • 0.75 cup Light Brown Sugar (Packed firmly for a deep caramel flavor)
  • 0.5 cup Granulated White Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg + 1 Egg Yolk (Room temperature; extra yolk adds signature richness)
  • 2 teaspoons Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 2.25 cups All-Purpose Flour (Sifted)
  • 1 teaspoon Cornstarch (The secret step for an ultra-soft, tender center)
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon Salt
  • 1.5 cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips or Chunks (Plus extra to press into cookie tops)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Blend the Sugars and Fats: In a large mixing bowl, whisk the melted unsalted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until no brown sugar lumps remain.
  2. Emulsify the Wet Base: Whisk in the whole egg, extra egg yolk, and pure vanilla extract. Continue beating vigorously by hand for 1 minute until the mixture turns smooth, glossy, and slightly pale.
  3. Sift Dry Components: In a separate bowl, stir together the sifted all-purpose flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and fold gently with a silicone spatula until a thick dough forms.
  4. Fold the Chocolate: Pour in the semi-sweet chocolate chips and gently fold them into the dough, ensuring they are evenly distributed.
  5. Portion and Tall-Shape: Scoop large balls of dough (about 3 tablespoons each). Instead of rolling them into perfect spheres, shape them into tall, upright cylinders. Tall dough mounds spread less in the oven, preserving a thick, chewy heart.
  6. The Crucial Chill: Place the dough portions onto a tray, cover tightly with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (up to 3 days). Do not skip this step, or the melted butter will cause the cookies to spread flat.
  7. Bake to Soft Perfection: Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Space the chilled dough cylinders 2 inches apart. Bake for 11 to 13 minutes. The edges should look set and barely golden, while the centers must look slightly soft and underbaked.
  8. Top and Cool: Remove from the oven and immediately press a few extra chocolate chips directly into the warm tops for a beautiful presentation. Let them rest on the hot pan for 10 minutes to finish setting before transferring to a wire rack to cool down completely.
