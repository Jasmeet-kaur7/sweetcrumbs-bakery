BLACK FROST PASTRY

The Black Forest Pastry is a decadent dessert consisting of alternating layers of moist chocolate cake and heavenly whipped cream. It typically features three layers of chocolate sponge soaked in a sweet cherry syrup and finished with additional whipped cream, chocolate shavings, and a whole cherry on top.

INGREDIENTS

For the Chocolate Sponge:

  • All-purpose flour (approx. 1 ½ cups)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (½ cup)
  • Granulated sugar (¾ to 1 cup)
  • Baking soda (1 tsp) and Salt (½ tsp)
  • Liquid base: Buttermilk, or a mix of water/milk with vinegar
  • Vegetable oil and Vanilla or chocolate extract

For Filling and Topping:

  • Heavy whipping cream and confectioners' sugar
  • Pitted black cherries or maraschino cherries
  • Cherry syrup (made from sugar, water, and cherries)
  • Dark chocolate shavings for garnish

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Sponge: Preheat oven to 350°F. Whisk dry ingredients together, then combine with wet ingredients until smooth. Bake in a greased pan (typically 9x5 inch for pastries or round for cakes) until a toothpick comes out clean.
  2. Prepare Cherry Syrup: Simmer sliced cherries with water and sugar for 4-5 minutes until a syrup forms. Let it cool completely.
  3. Make Whipped Cream: Using an electric mixer, beat cold heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  4. Assemble: Once the cake is cool, slice it into layers. Lightly brush each layer with the cherry syrup. Spread a layer of whipped cream and add chopped cherries. Repeat for subsequent layers.
  5. Finish: Frost the top and sides with remaining cream. Garnish with chocolate shavings and whole cherries.
