COLD BREW AVOCADO SHAKE

A rich, velvety, and energizing specialty fusion shake crafted exclusively for Father's Day. It perfectly balances the creamy, buttery goodness of fresh ripe avocados with a clean, powerful splash of slow-steeped smooth cold brew coffee.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large ripe avocado (scooped and pitted)
  • 100 ml concentrated smooth cold brew coffee
  • 120 ml chilled whole milk (or condensed milk for extra sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons organic honey or simple syrup
  • 1 cup of crushed ice or frozen milk cubes
  • Fresh avocado slices (for side garnish presentation)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Slice the fresh ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the rich green flesh.
  2. Add the scooped avocado flesh, chilled whole milk, honey, and ice cubes directly into a high-speed blender.
  3. Blend on high for 30 to 45 seconds until the mixture hits an exceptionally smooth, thick, and velvety texture.
  4. Pour the robust cold brew coffee concentrate straight into the base of a tall serving glass.
  5. Slowly layer the thick blended avocado shake over the cold brew coffee base to create a beautiful dual-tone effect.
  6. Garnish with a fresh avocado slice on the rim and serve instantly with a wide straw.
