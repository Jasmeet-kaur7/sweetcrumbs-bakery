VANILLA PASTRY

This sophisticated Vanilla Pastry features layers of exceptionally light, moist vanilla sponge cake paired with a smooth, velvety whipped vanilla buttercream. Textured frosting waves cover the top surface, perfectly complemented by a scatter of shimmering golden sugar pearls. Cut into pristine, uniform rectangles, this treat brings high-end bakery style to any special occasion.

INGREDIENTS

    For the Delicate Vanilla Sponge:

    • 250g Fine cake flour
    • 200g Caster sugar
    • 1½ tsp Baking powder
    • ¼ tsp Salt
    • 150g Unsalted butter (softened)
    • 3 Large eggs (room temperature)
    • 120ml Whole milk
    • 2 tsp Premium vanilla bean extract
    • 30ml Light sugar syrup (for moistening)

    For the Buttercream & Topping:

    • 200g Unsalted butter (softened)
    • 400g Icing sugar (double sifted)
    • 30ml Heavy cream
    • 1 tsp Clear vanilla extract (for white frosting)
    • 2 tbsp Edible golden sugar pearls/sprinkles

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bake the Base: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a large, flat rectangular baking sheet with parchment paper. Beat butter and caster sugar together until pale. Add eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla. Alternately fold in the flour mixture and milk until perfectly smooth. Spread evenly into the sheet and bake for 18–20 minutes. Cool completely.
  2. Whip the Frosting: Whip the softened butter on high speed for 5 minutes until pale and airy. Gradually beat in the sifted icing sugar, heavy cream, and clear vanilla extract until light, fluffy, and spreadable.
  3. Layer the Cake: Slice the cooled flat vanilla sheet cake cleanly into two identical rectangles. Lightly brush the surfaces with sugar syrup to lock in moisture.
  4. Assemble and Texture: Spread a smooth layer of vanilla buttercream over the first cake slice. Place the second layer on top. Coat the upper surface with a generous layer of buttercream. Use a notched cake comb or a small spatula to create wave-like textures across the top surface.
  5. Slice and Finish: Place the cake in the freezer for 15 minutes to firm up. Using a hot, sharp knife, trim the edges cleanly and slice into uniform rectangular pastries. Garnish the top of each pastry with a scattering of golden sugar pearls.
